Vegan Crepes

Delicious and light vegan crepes perfect for any filling!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups almond milk (or any plant-based milk)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, almond milk, vegetable oil, sugar, vanilla extract, and salt until smooth.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly grease it with oil.
  3. Pour about 1/4 cup of the batter into the skillet, tilting the pan to spread the batter evenly into a thin layer.
  4. Cook for about 1-2 minutes until the edges start to lift and the bottom is lightly golden.
  5. Flip the crepe and cook for another 1-2 minutes until golden on the other side.
  6. Repeat with the remaining batter, stacking the crepes on a plate.
  7. Serve warm with your favorite fillings such as fruits, vegan chocolate spread, or maple syrup.

Enjoy your delicious vegan crepes!
