Zucchini Soup

A delicious plant based soup for those hot summer nights!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 medium zucchinis, sliced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onion and minced garlic, saut√© until translucent.
  3. Add sliced zucchinis and cook for about 5 minutes.
  4. Pour in vegetable broth and bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat and simmer for 15-20 minutes until zucchinis are tender.
  6. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil.
