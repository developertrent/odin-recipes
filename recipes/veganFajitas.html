Vegan Fajitas

Spicy and flavorful vegan fajitas for a perfect meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add sliced bell pepper and onion, saut√© until soft.
  3. Add minced garlic, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper; cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Warm the tortillas in a separate pan or microwave.
  5. Serve the vegetable mixture in tortillas, garnished with fresh cilantro.

Enjoy your delicious vegan fajitas!
